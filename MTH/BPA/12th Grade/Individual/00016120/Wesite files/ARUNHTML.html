What is BPA?

BPA is an organization that provides students, who are interested in business related careers, with skills that they can use in future careers, as well as in their personal lives. BPA's mission is to develop and empower student leaders to discover their passion and change the world by creating unmatched opportunities in learning, professional growth and service. Students can show what they know by competing in business subjects locally and nationally.

Subjects to Compete In

  • Finance
  • Business Administration
  • Management Information Systems
  • Digital Communication & Design
  • Management, Marketing & Communication

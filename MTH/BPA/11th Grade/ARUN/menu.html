Specialty Pizzas
Bacon Chicken Ranch Pizza A common specialty pizza made with the Russo Family Twist. The Bacon Chicken Ranch pizza is made with fresh dough. A shredded chicken, ranching dredding mix is placed on top of the pizza dough as a sauce. We cover the top of the pizza with Russo's favorites, crispy bacon, sliced green onions, diced tomatoes, chopped celery, and red peppers. The pizza is then topped with cheese mixture including, mozarella, cheddar cheese, and Colby. $14.00
Bacon Spinach Tomato Our Bacon-Spinach-Tomato pizza takes your traditional bacon lettuce and tomato sandwich and turn it into a unique and delicious pizza. The freshly made pizza dough is covered with our white cream sauce, topped with crispy bacon, spinach, and large tomato slices. Parmesan and Asiago cheese sprinkled on top and cooked to perfection. $12.00
Blue Cheese Prosciutto The Blue Cheese Prosciutto Pizza is the main pizza that has been passed through the Russo family before the business even started. The Blue Cheese pizza started in Venice and made it over to the United States, when the family migrated. The freshly-made pizza dough is topped off with the family secret tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, blue cheese crumbles, and prosciutto. The pizza is baked until crispy and fluffy.
Greek Pizza Here is a chance to try your favorite salad as pizza. The Greek Pizza is made with freshly-made whole wheat dough and seasoned, whole-crushed tomatoes sauce. The sauce is topped with with spinach, sliced red onions, halved Kalamata olives, sliced red peppers, feta cheese, and mozzarella cheese. After the pizza is cooked to perfection, our family-secret Greek dressing on top for that extra Greek salad flavor
Margherita Pizza Our Margherita is made with fresh ingredients from beginning to end. The Homemade dough is topped with our family secret Pesto sauce, slices of fresh Roma tomatoes, black olives, and large slices of Mozzarella cheese melted to a bubbly perfection. $10.00
Mediterranean Pizza Our Number one choice of speciality pizza, according to our customers is the refreshing Mediterranean Pizza. Similar to Margherita pizza, the Mediterranean Pizza uses our house specialty pesto sauce. Topped with sun-dried tomatoes, artichoke hearts, grilled chicken breasts, kalamata olives, mushrooms, red onions, goat cheese, feta cheese, basil leaves, spinach, and shredded mozzarella cheese. $14.00
